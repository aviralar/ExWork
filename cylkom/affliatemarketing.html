Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing arrangement by which an online retailer pays commmision to an external website for traffic or sales generated from its referrals. "Most of my earnings are from affiliate marketing, direct ad sales and sponsered posts"
Customer visits publisher website
Customer sees advertising banner/link and clicks through via an affiliate tracking link. The customer will likely not realise they have used an affiliate link
The click is tracked by the affiliate network and a cookie is placed on the customer's device to identify them as being referred by an affiliate network
The customer completes an online purchase
The affiliate fires the affiliate network's tracking tag which reports the sale to affiliate network as there is a cookie present
The affiliate network reports the sale to the advertiser and they pay the agreed commission for that sale
The affiliate network pays the commision to the publisher as a reward for generating the sale
